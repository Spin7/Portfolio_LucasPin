About Me

Engineer, Researcher & Educator

Based in Asunción, Paraguay · Born 2001 · Paraguayan

I am a Mechatronic Engineer graduating from the Faculty of Engineering at the Universidad Nacional de Asunción with a GPA of 4.82/5 and an Honor Roll distinction for 2026.

My thesis, "Application of Deep Learning and Agent-Based Modeling for the Development of an Intelligent Mosquito Trap", combines computer vision with agent-based simulation to address real-world public health problems.

I currently work as a Research Assistant at the Laboratory of Computer Science and Applied Engineering (LCIAE), where I work on CNN model training, edge computing, and complex systems simulation.
