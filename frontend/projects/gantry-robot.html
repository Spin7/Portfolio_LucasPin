Egg Inspection Gantry Robot

Completed2024
🥚

Overview

Development of a three-degree-of-freedom gantry robot equipped with computer vision for the automatic detection and classification of defective eggs on a conveyor belt. The system performs quality control autonomously.

C++OpenCVComputer VisionGantry RobotControl Systems
GitHub →
DOF3 degrees of freedom
ApplicationAutomated quality control

Detailed Description

The gantry robot moves along three axes (X, Y, Z) over a conveyor belt. A camera mounted on the end-effector captures images of each egg. Computer vision algorithms analyze color, shape, and surface anomalies to classify eggs as good or defective.

When a defective egg is detected, the robot arm moves to the target position and activates a removal mechanism. The system was designed and built from scratch, including mechanical structure, electronics, and software.
