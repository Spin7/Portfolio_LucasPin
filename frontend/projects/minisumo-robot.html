Overview

Detailed Description

The minisumo robot was fully designed in SolidWorks before manufacturing. The chassis was optimized for low center of gravity and high traction, with carefully placed internal assemblies for motors, sensors, and electronics.

The robot uses proximity and line sensors for autonomous sumo strategy: detecting the opponent and pushing them out of the ring while staying within bounds. The design was validated against minisumo weight and size regulations.
