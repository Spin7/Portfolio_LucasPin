Linear Algebra

Undergraduate Teaching Assistant FIUNA

Second-semester undergraduate course covering the core foundations of linear algebra: vector spaces, linear transformations, matrix decompositions, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, and inner product spaces. The course emphasizes both rigorous proofs and practical computational applications in engineering contexts.

Taught as Teaching Assistant (Profesor Auxiliar) at the Facultad de Ingeniería — Universidad Nacional de Asunción (FIUNA). Responsible for weekly problem-solving sessions, grading, and preparation of supplementary materials.
